{{ page.title }}: A Hybrid Approach for Near-Range Video Stabilization

Achievement

  • We improved traditional 3D motion estimation model by introducing infinite-homography based motion estimation. We demonstrate that this improvement would not sacrifice too much stabilizing effects, but avoid significant content distortion for near-range videos.
  • We present a hybrid approach that combines the benefits of 2D methods with those of improved 3D methods for near-range video stabilization.
